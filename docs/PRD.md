# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Objectives & Scope
StadiumIQ aims to deliver tournament coordinators and stadium operators a central intelligence dashboard that provides:
- Predictive crowd surge alerts (Gemini/Vertex AI or deterministic fallbacks).
- Round-robin schedule optimization respecting minimum rest thresholds.
- Dynamic visual digital replication of stand density (Digital Twin).

## 2. Key User Flows
- **Dashboard Overview**: Accessing metrics on active gates, current filling percentages, and warning lists.
- **Bracket Generation**: Submitting list of competing teams to receive conflict-free schedulings.
- **Safety Actions**: Activating auxiliary pathways or dispatching coordinators based on gate utilization peaks.
